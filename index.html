About #include

Founded in 2016
We handle the web stuff so nonprofits can focus on doing good

In 2016, #include was born with the objective of putting together robust web and mobile applications and platforms for nonprofit organizations. Soon after our humble beginnings, we harvested a diverse team made up of the passionate students part of the UC Davis community.

By working in our specially crafted, intricate team structure, we are able to build projects from the ground up, tailoring projects to the needs of the causes we care about from all aspects of the design and engineering processes.

Our Mission

To code beautifully engineered and practical websites for the community.

To never JUST develop, we adhere to a very meticulous process of marketing, designing and coding.

To always think outside of the box, and maintain our team values.
